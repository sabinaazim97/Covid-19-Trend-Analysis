Death Distribution by Country

As seen in this analysis, the United States lost over 700,000 individuals throughout the COVID-19 pandemic. Brazil came in second with over 600,000 deaths, and India follows with over 450,000 deaths.

New Deaths Per Month

This analysis shows how deaths were distributed throughout reported continents. North America was leading in number of deaths for the beginning of 2021; however, it started to go down from May 2021-July 2021. Asia took a drastic lead in the number of deaths during the summer of 2021. However, Asia's death rates started decreasing again in August 2021. South America also saw a spike in COVID related deaths during the summer of 2021; however, like Asia, the death rates started slowing down again in August 2021.
